Education

At GreenHands, we believe that education is key to building a more sustainable future. We offer a variety of educational resources and programs to help people of all ages learn about the environment and how to protect it.



Programs

  • GreenHands in the Classroom: Our team of educators provides interactive and engaging environmental education lessons for K-12 students. We work with teachers to tailor our programs to their curriculum and classroom needs.
  • GreenHands Youth Corps: Our youth leadership program empowers teens to take action on environmental issues in their communities. Participants develop leadership skills, learn about local ecosystems, and plan and implement their own environmental projects.
  • GreenHands Community Workshops: We offer workshops for adults on a variety of topics, including sustainable gardening, green home improvements, and waste reduction.


Resources



  • GreenHands Library: Our online library provides a wealth of information on a variety of environmental topics, from climate change to sustainable agriculture. Visitors can browse books, articles, and videos, and download resources for free.
  • GreenHands Blog: Our blog features stories and insights from our team of experts, as well as guest contributors. Topics range from the latest environmental news to tips for living a more eco-friendly lifestyle.
  • GreenHands Newsletter: Our monthly newsletter features updates on our programs and projects, as well as news and resources on environmental issues. Sign up to stay informed!
